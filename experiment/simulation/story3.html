Relay
Relays are switches that open and close circuits electromechanically or electronically.
Circuit Breaker
To interrupt the current flow after a fault is detected.
Slip Ring Induction Motor
Generally employed where load requires high starting torque or good speed control.
Squirrel Cage Induction Motor
It functions on the principle of electromagnetism.The interaction of the magnetic fields produced by the stator and rotor windings produces a torque on the squirrel cage rotor.
DC Motor
A DC motor is any of a class of rotary electrical machines that converts direct current electrical energy into mechanical energy.
Auto Transformer
It is used to start induction motors,regulate the voltage of transmission lines,transform voltages when the primary to secondary ratio is close to unity.
